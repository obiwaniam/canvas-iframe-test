Quick Warm-Up

This study break reinforces core vocabulary and function relationships from the skeletal and muscular systems. Use it as a focused review before quizzes, labs, or discussion posts.

  • Identify key bones from short descriptions
  • Connect major muscles to their actions
  • Practice spelling and recall of high-value A&P terms

How To Use This Activity

Complete each mini-game in order or jump between tabs. You will earn a badge for each game and unlock a personalized completion code after all three are done.

Tip: Say each answer out loud as you play. Verbal recall can improve retention before your next assignment or exam.
